Evidence protocol

  1. Understanding your question
  2. Finding original evidence
  3. Checking repeated claims
  4. Examining source incentives
  5. Looking for credible contradictions
  6. Identifying uncertainty
  7. Building your answer

Answer ingredients

Primary evidence, systematic review, professional guidance, commercial source checked, repetition collapsed.

Visibility is not authority.

NOT GPT does not treat the most visible pages as the strongest evidence.

Repetition is not proof.

Twenty pages repeating one claim are not twenty independent pieces of evidence.

GOO stays separate.

GOO opportunities never enter NOT GPT answers, rankings, citations or prompts.
